Afterhours Pick-up

If you need after hour service pick-up there is a 15,000 KRW fee and you will need to:

To make your self-driving vacation in Jeju go even smoother, we allow you to pick up your car from our agency’s parking lot after 20:00. However, if you choose this option, you must return your car during normal business hours (07:30–20:00) at our Tamra rental agency – not the airport. This is because we will need to conduct a full inspection of your vehicle.

If you want to use our after-hours pick-up service you will need to:

1. Purchase full insurance from Tamra Rent a Car, or if you are a member of the U.S. Armed Forces Korea and currently have USAA that covers you for car rentals in South Korea (please check your policy beforehand). Or you have your own car rental car insurance from your credit card company or travel insurance - please check with your insurance company beforehand. Some insurance companies require customers to buy car rental CDW’s beforehand in order to cover you in the event an accident occurs.

Please note that if you want to use your own insurance you will be responsible for 100% for all fees relating to the accident. We do not file claims on behalf of our customers via their insurance providers. Therefore, in the event of an accident, you will pay the necessary fees at Tamra and we will provide you with your contract and the necessary documentation to make a claim.

2. Provide us with a copy of your Passport, International Driving Permit and or a Korean License/ USFK License, or your Residence Permit (if differs from your IDP issuing country).

3. Fill out and sign a credit card authorization form and provide a photo of your credit card (side with card number and expiration date). Otherwise we cannot provide you with our after-hours pick-up service.

Please note we do not allow any customer to drop off the car outside of normal business hours. We will charge you a $250USD fine for violating our rules. We need our car returned during normal business hours so we can ensure that it does not have any problems and has been brought back with the correct amount of fuel and its original state.

When you pick-up your car, please take as many photos as you can before you take the car. We already have pictures of the car’s damage loaded into our system. In the event you get into an accident, we will use both ours and your pictures to compare photos.

If you plan to extend your car rental period, please, do so at the time of pick-up to avoid delayed responses, insurance problems and late fees. Please contact us if you are going to be late. When you return the car, please, come to the counter to pay your late fees. Extra rental fees and insurance charges will apply. In addition, you might have to return to our car rental agency to switch cars if your car has been booked by another person ahead of time. Vehicle rentals and returns are only available during our working hours (7 days a week from 07:30 to 20:00).

IF YOU DON’T NOTIFY US ABOUT BEING LATE, A 50,000 KRW PENALTY FEE WILL BE CHARGED FOR EVERY EXTRA HOUR!
